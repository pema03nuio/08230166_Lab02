Education

Current Education

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Education in Information Technology (B.Ed IT) at Samtse College of Education, under the Royal University of Bhutan (RUB). Being in my third year of study, I am deeply engaged in learning both theoretical and practical aspects of ICT, alongside the pedagogical skills required to become a competent teacher. This program has not only enhanced my knowledge of technology but has also shaped me into a reflective learner who aspires to inspire future generations.

Higher Secondary Education

From 2019 to 2021, I studied at Bartsham Central School, where I successfully completed my Higher Secondary education. My time there was filled with valuable learning experiences, both academic and co-curricular. I had the honor of being appointed as the class captain in Grade 11 and later the house captain in Grade 12, roles that helped me develop leadership qualities, responsibility, and a spirit of service. These experiences left a lasting impact on my confidence and ability to work collaboratively with peers and teachers alike.

Lower Secondary Education

My educational foundation was built at Bidung Lower Secondary School, from Pre-Primary to 10th year, where I studied from 2008 to 2018. The school provided me with not only academic basics but also values of humility, perseverance, and respect for others. The friendships, teachers, and experiences from Bidung school remain an integral part of my journey, shaping the student and individual I am today.
